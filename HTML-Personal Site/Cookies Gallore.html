Peanut Butter Oatmeal Protein Cookies

These healthy, Peanut Butter Oatmeal Protein Cookies are made with bananas, oats and chocolate PLUS protein powder, peanut butter and egg added for protein.

Bannana Oat Protein Cookie Ingredients:

  • Bananas:You'll want to use very ripe bananas - the ripper the bananas, the sweeter the cookies.
  • Oats:Use old-fashion oats, or quick oats.
  • Nut Butter:You can use peanut butter, or you can also use almound butter or seed butter if you have nut allergies.
  • Vanilla Protein Powder:1 scoop of your favorite vanilla protein powder.
  • Egg:One large egg has six grams of protein.
  • Salt:a pinch of kosher salt.
  • Flavor:Cinnamon and vanilla make these breakfast Cookies smell and taste amazing.
  • Chocolate Chip Cookies:I use Lily's sugar-free chocolate chips, but feel free to use regular semi-sweet chips.

How to store, freeze, and reheat

These healthy protein cookies hold up quite well and will even last for 4 days in the fridge. They can be eaten at room temperature or warmed in the microwave for 10 seconds.
You can also freeze these cookies for up to three months and thaw for a few hours in the fridge or microwave straight from the freezer.

Protein Cookie Variations:

  • Swap peanut butter for almound or cashew butter or sunflower butter for a nut-free cookie.
  • Switch out the chocolate chips for rasins or dried cranberries.
  • Add chopped pecans or walnuts for extra protein.
